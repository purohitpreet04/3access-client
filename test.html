Understanding risk factors, triggers and how to reduce the risk.

For each risk with a score of 5 or above, discuss how and why you reached that decision, and what action plan can be agreed to reduce the risk.

By understanding your risks you can help reduce the likelihood of them happening and help keep you and others you live with safe.

If during your stay with us you feel your risk rating has increased or reduced, please discuss this with your support worker and together you can amend the risk management plan as needed.

If your support worker feels that your risk rating needs to be amended, they will discuss this with you at your support session.

This risk assessment will be reviewed every 3 months at your Quarterly Review support session
